  • Jo Voordeckers

    Jo Voordeckers

    Jo is no newcomer to the Java community, at JavaOne'08 he spoke on how to implement streaming video in the pre-release of JavaFX and also developed the Parleys.com JavaFX demo for the JavaOne'08 keynote. Next to his day job as a Java, web and mobile developer for Parleys.com and Devoxx, he is active in the Devoxx steering committee and the Belgium JUG where he drives the coding dojo sessions. Jo spends most of his free time experimenting with new technologies and enjoys speaking about Swing, JavaFX, Flex, Mercurial/GIT and lately HTML5. Jo has presented at JavaOne, Devoxx and BeJUG, blogs at blog.pursuit.be
